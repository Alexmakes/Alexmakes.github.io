Sub 40 Min Benchy with ender 3v2 for under £20 

alex benett

the old ender 3v2 needed replacement parts mainly its extruder was broken and the hotend was getting clogged every other print. so maybe it was a time for upgrade and without breaking the bank 

Hardware needed 

  1. a geared extruder 
  2. bi metal hotend

the cheap all metal geared extruders for the ender 3 are cheap and allow for the filament to be pushed harder and retreated faster, price £8 free shipping, the Bi metal hotend is magic the bottom made of titanium of the hotend remains hot allowing for high flow rates but the top made of copper tranmits the heat away from the filament preventing heat creep. 

software 

the ender 3 bowden tube is known for causing issues with clogging and resistant too pressure change that retraction and changes in speed of extruding cause. so we want to keep the pressure in the tube as constant as possible. we do this with automatic speed on prusia slicer you have measure the max flow rate of the hotend mine maxed out at 8mm^2/s with PLA, then slicer takes care of the rest.

the steppers on the ender 3v2 have a lot more torque than you'll expect so its simple to go into the firmware and up the acceleration until it didn't skip.
